Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

SafeJourney ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our safety services, we collect the following information:

  • Location Data: We collect precise location data to enable real-time tracking and safety broadcasts. This is essential for the app's core functionality.
  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Contact Information: We access your contacts only when you explicitly choose to add them as trusted safety contacts.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide real-time journey tracking features.
  • Enable you to share your location with chosen contacts.
  • Send push notifications related to your journey.
  • Improve and maintain the application's performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your location is only shared with the specific contacts you authorize during a "Live Broadcast" session. We may use third-party services like Firebase for push notifications and analytics.

4. Data Retention

We retain your journey data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@safejourney.app.
